About Ralson

70+ Years of
Tyre Excellence

Ralson India Limited, headquartered in Ludhiana, Punjab, is a name synonymous with quality, precision and innovation in the global tyre industry. From a small domestic enterprise, we've grown into one of India's most trusted tyre manufacturers.

With state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, a dedicated R&D team, and an unrelenting commitment to quality, Ralson continues to set new benchmarks in tyre technology — from featherweight bicycle tyres to heavy-duty commercial giants.
